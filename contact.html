FAQ

Common Questions

Is travel funding available? +

The announcement does not mention travel funding. Please contact the organizers directly at consciousness.commons@gmail.com to ask about travel support.

Can I apply without prior experience with Cogitate datasets? +

Yes. You need sufficient technical capacity to analyse data, or a strong willingness to develop these skills during the project.

Do I need to attend ASSC 2026 to participate? +

No — Consciousness Commons is a satellite event of ASSC, but attending the main conference is not required. You only need to be available July 4–5 in Santiago and for the pre-event virtual kick-off in May.

When will I hear back after applying? +

All applicants will be notified between April 22–27, 2026.
