Key Dates

What's Coming Up

Phase 1 — Applications
March 23, 2026

Application Window Opens

The application form goes live. Submit your application via Google Forms. See eligibility criteria →

April 13, 2026

Application Deadline

Final day to submit your application. Late submissions will not be considered. Applications close at end of day.

April 22–27, 2026

Applicants Notified

All applicants will be contacted with a decision during this window. Accepted participants will receive onboarding instructions.

Phase 2 — Pre-Event
May 11–16, 2026

Pre-Event Virtual Kick-off

A virtual session to begin the Mind-Match team-facilitation process and complete light onboarding before arriving in Santiago. Exact date within this window to be confirmed.

Phase 3 — Workshop
July 4–5, 2026

⭐ Consciousness Commons — Santiago

Two days of intensive collaborative science in Santiago, Chile, immediately following ASSC 2026. Teams form, formulate hypotheses, select datasets, build analysis plans, and draft OSF preregistrations.

Phase 4 — Post-Workshop
July 5–12, 2026

Award Proposal Submission Deadline

Teams wishing to compete for prize funding must submit their formal proposal and OSF preregistration within one week of the workshop closing.

July 20, 2026

Award Announcement

Prize winners announced. Up to $25,000 in seed funding distributed across winning teams to support continued research.

August 10 – November 8, 2026

Project Period

Funded teams carry out their preregistered research projects with support from brainlife.io and the broader Consciousness Commons community.
